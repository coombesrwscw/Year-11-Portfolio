Implications

Asthetics

I will try to keep a theme of balance & war between technology and nature by making about half of the character look robotic/weaponized, and making the other half look natural, and texturing it with things in keeping with the theme. (e.g. bark, bone, leaves, ect...)

Functionality

I will try to double check every time I make an edit to the design, and I will do this by making a habit of at least checking my changes at the end of each editing session.

Usability

I will try to make sure that my character is easy to use by making sure that the rigging is simple and easy to understand, and that the model is not too heavy on the computer.
